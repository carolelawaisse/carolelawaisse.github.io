MY ARTWORK TRAVELING ABOUT ME

Artist, Traveler and Lover

As an art student at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent, I'm working day after day in my studio. Every morning I get up with this gigantic urge to paint.
I also want to go on an exploration and get to know every corner of our planet. As I am only 20 years old and I am still studying, I am not yet in a position to obtain initial capital to travel for a long period of time. For this reason, my friend and I are working in our studio every day during the academic year and we spend our holidays on paid work. Then we leave as long as possible in September. So doing nothing is not an option.
Once we have our start capital, we want our art to be influenced even more by our travels. We want to be blown over by all inspiration and learn as much as possible about other cultures.
We can only do this after our studies, so until that time we will give everything we can to learn as much as possible about painting right now.
