ABOUT MY ARTWORK ABOUT TRAVELING ABOUT ME

Artist, Traveler and Lover

I study arts in Ghent.
But that doesn't mean I want to stay here
or that I want to become famous.
The reason that I study paintings is because
I can, because I have the talent
and the will to do it gives me the reason I must use it.
My dream is to use this quality and travel around the world with it.
I want to discover new experiences and use it in my artwork.
I want to help people with what I can.
But to do all of that, I have to travel the world.
