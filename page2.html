About my art


Since I want to integrate my travels into my paintings in the future, I am now fully engaged in learning various techniques. I do this through simple objects, the choices of my life that attract my attention and visualize them. I also find it more interesting to make installations myself and then work on them. Of course, my works are far from how I would like them to be but to learn, I have to start small.
