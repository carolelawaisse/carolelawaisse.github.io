About Traveling

As I am still studying, I can't leave for too long.
But me and my boyfriend, we try to travel as
much as we can. We make a lot of small trips
and once a year, we try to go to the other side
of our planet. This year we went to Ecuador.

'Why does everybody always say 'and then we go home'?'

-Mabelle Frias

Me and my boyfriend love to travel. Last year we went to Ecuador together, which was amazing.
The first few days, we stayed in Quito, then we left to go the Amazon in Cuyabeno National Reserve. Which was such a strange experience but we would do it all over again. We did night walks, we looked for alligators, we even got lost for four hours but it was one of the most beautiful places I had ever seen. 
After that we went to Mindo, which was literally paradise on earth. We saw some beautiful waterfalls and so many birds.
But the best part was Otavalo. Where we stayed on a little farm called El Tio. The owners were so nice. They asked us to make bread with them but we were a little to tired. We also met a lovely family who traveled the world with theire children. I had never seen children so smart as those two and parents who take so much time to learn their children everything they want to know. 
In Otavalo, we visited the Cuicocha Lake, wich was my favorite place, it was so quite and so peacefull. All the colours just blended in so perfectly. After that we visited the Peguche waterfall where we had a great laugh because I wasn't able to guess how big it was.
The day after we went to the biggest market in South-America. We bought so many nice stuff. But also, we saw the animal market. I had this double feeling about it because they don't treat the animals like they should but on the other hand it's way better than in the factories. How ever, after that we had to leave. We both didn't want to leave this pleace because it simply felt like home. Happely, the family was so nice to take us to the busstation.
That evening we arrived in Latacunga. We ate well and prepaired our selfs to climb Quilotoa the next they. I was pretty scared that I would fall but my sweetest boyfriend helped me whenever I asked him. Quilotoa was magical, it's hard to explain how beautifull it was. There was this atmosphere that I had never felt before. We stayed there for lunch and then we went back up, riding a duncky. 
The next day was my boyfriends favorite, he climbed the Cotopaxi. I stayed in the car, waiting for him because that was something he had to do on his own. When he came back, he was so happy. He told me about the wind, how cold is but how wonderfull at the same time. I was so proud of him, also because he climbed it in only 20 minutes. 
The day after, we were already on the plane to Manta, where someone picked us up and drove us to Puerto Lopez. My boyfriend got really ill for one day because so we stayed at the hotel, watched some movies, lisened to music and hoped that we could go whale watching the day after. Wich we indeed did. We saw so many whales, it was unbelieveble. The day after, we saw even more of them, we even saw some killer whales. Also we went to Isla De La Plata but we didn't like that so much. But back in the hostel, we had such a nice evening together.
On our way back to Belgium, we felt so bad. We didn't have the feeling that we were returning back home but we felt like, we left home.
